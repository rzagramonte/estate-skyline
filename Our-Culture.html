Our Culture

Supporting our community and beyond

Social Responsibility

Estate Skyline LLP maintains an ongoing commitment to charitable work through programs that give back to the youth in New York. As an advocate for the YMCA of Greater New York, Estate Skyline LLP annually hosts teen interns through YMCA’s Teen Career Connection Summer Internship Program, which provides teens from low-income communities with the opportunity to work in professional environments and gain access to career training. We also welcome high-need students as interns through the PENCIL Fellows Program.

Estate Skyline LLP supports additional programs that directly supply thousands of children living in New York City homeless and domestic violence shelters with school supplies and holiday toys, including Operation Backpack, sponsored by Volunteers of America Greater New York, and Winter Wishes, sponsored by New York Cares.

Advancing Diversity & Inclusion

Individuality is both highly valued and highly celebrated at Estate Skyline LLP. We are an equal opportunity employer with an ongoing commitment to cultivating a diverse work environment, offering opportunities to all qualified persons regardless of race, color, religion, sex, age, national origin, marital status, veteran status, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity and expression, or status in any group protected by federal, state or local law. R&E’s senior legal management includes gender, ethnic and LGBTQ diversity, and women, persons of color (POC) and members of the LGBTQ community are represented in management of a number of critical areas of Firm services.

A Greener New York

In 2019, Estate Skyline LLP was recognized by the New York City Bar’s Environmental Law Committee for our efforts in reducing our environmental footprint. The Firm’s recent participation in New York State Energy Research & Development Authority (NYSERDA)’s Commercial Tenant Program is a testament to our commitment to reducing energy consumption, making the business case for energy efficient workspaces and setting the example for other law firms to follow.
