Jocelyn N. Rosales

Paralegal

Jocelyn Rosales is a paralegal at the firm focusing on residential and commercial real state, and commercial leases. Before joining Estate Skyline LLP, she worked on several immigration law projects helping attorneys with immigrant survivors of crime, torture, or abuse in New York City.

Jocelyn completed her Paralegal Studies degree at St. John's University School of Law in Queens, New York. Her academic and professional experience has introduced her to common and civil law legal systems.
